## Quick Actions

### Song Length
- **Set Exact:** Enter measures â†’ Click "Set"
- **Extend:** Click "+4" to add 4 measures
- **Trim:** Click "Trim" to fit tracks exactly

### Grid Snapping
- **Toggle:** Check/uncheck "Snap Enabled"
- **Divisions:** Measure, 1/2, 1/4, 1/8, 1/16, Triplet, Off
- **Default:** 1/4 Note (1 beat)

### Markers
- **Add:** Click "+ Add Marker" â†’ Enter beat â†’ Select type
- **Delete:** Click on marker in timeline
- **Types:** Intro, Verse, Chorus, Bridge, Solo, Breakdown, Build, Outro

### Tempo Automation
- **Add Point:** Click "+ Add Tempo Point" â†’ Enter beat & BPM
- **Delete Point:** Click tempo point marker in timeline
- **Range:** 40-240 BPM

## Loop Region

1. Check "Loop Region" in Playback Controls
2. Set "Start" beat (e.g., 0)
3. Set "End" beat (e.g., 16)
4. Blue bracket appears on timeline
5. Press Play â†’ Music loops

```

3. Add Intro, Verse, Chorus markers
```
