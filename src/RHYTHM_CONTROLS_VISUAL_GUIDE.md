## â“ FAQ

**Q: Why don't I see rhythm controls for counterpoint?**  
A: Counterpoint uses automatic rhythm from species counterpoint algorithms. No manual controls needed.

**Q: Can I collapse rhythm controls?**  
A: Yes! Click the header to expand/collapse (theme & Bach variables). Imitation/fugue rhythm controls are always visible in their cards.

**Q: How do I know if my rhythm changes are saved?**  
A: Rhythm data is automatically saved in app state. Play the composition to verify your changes.

**Q: Do rhythm controls work with MIDI input?**  
A: Yes! Notes added via MIDI keyboard inherit the current rhythm pattern or default to quarter notes.
